# Safety and Legal Disclaimer

> **Exploratory prototype documentation**

SynAptIp Scientific Interoperability Platform is an independent engineering and interoperability initiative. This repository is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any equipment manufacturer.

This repository currently contains exploratory design documentation, templates, and starter CAD logic only. It does not establish validated compatibility, validated safety, validated tolerances, validated analytical performance, or production readiness.

## Public-safety publication rules

Do not publish:

- personal identification documents
- confidential institutional information
- restricted laboratory data
- passwords, credentials, or API keys
- proprietary manufacturer files
- confidential supplier pricing
- serial numbers of sensitive equipment
- unpublished institutional research data
- unsafe chemical handling instructions
- hidden metadata from private source files
- original raw photographs
- private measurements that have not yet passed review

## Repository segregation

The public repository is limited to curated, sanitized, publication-safe outputs. Private local material includes original concept documents, raw photos, exploratory notes, unvalidated measurements, internal observations, early concept iterations, and raw exports.

No public file should be treated as evidence of manufacturer affiliation, endorsement, final compatibility, or completed validation.

## Use and review

Any future physical prototype, laboratory workflow, or compatibility statement must undergo appropriate human engineering review, risk assessment, and evidence-based validation before use or publication.
